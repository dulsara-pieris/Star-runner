# SYNAPSNEX Project Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We, the contributors and maintainers of the SYNAPSNEX project, pledge to:
- Respect the work of all creators.
- Give proper credit to original authors and contributors.
- Avoid misrepresentation or false claims about other projects or communities.
- Foster a positive and collaborative environment.

## Expected Behavior
All participants, whether using, modifying, or distributing this software, are expected to:
1. **Preserve attribution** of the original author (Dulsara Pieris) and any other contributors.
2. **Credit modifications** clearly and visibly.
3. **Avoid defaming, disparaging, or intentionally misrepresenting** other open-source projects, creators, or communities.
4. **Respect other users and contributors** in discussions, pull requests, and documentation.
5. **Follow license requirements**, including SAPL v1.3 obligations.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Removing or obscuring the original author’s credit.
- Claiming the work as entirely your own without proper attribution.
- Using this software to harass, defame, or misrepresent other open source projects or individuals.
- Violating license terms (SAPL v1.3) intentionally or recklessly.

## Enforcement
Violations of this Code of Conduct are taken seriously and may result in:
- Removal of contributions from the repository.
- Revocation of rights under the SAPL v1.3 license.
- Reporting to platforms (GitHub, etc.) for DMCA or misconduct enforcement.
